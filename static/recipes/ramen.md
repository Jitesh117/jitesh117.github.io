# Shin Ramyun Recipe

Spicy Shin Ramyun topped with crispy pan-fried chicken, jammy soft-boiled eggs, spring onions, and sesame seeds.

---

## Ingredients (1 serving)

### Base

- 1 packet **Shin Ramyun** (I used the Nongshim one)
- 500–550 ml water (slightly less than packet instructions for a richer broth)

### Toppings

- 60-100 g **chicken**, cut into small pieces.
- 1 **egg**
- 1 tsp **oil** (neutral or sesame)
- ¼ tsp **chili oil or gochujang** (optional, for extra heat)

### Garnish

- Spring onions, finely chopped
- White sesame seeds

---

## Instructions

### 1. Soft-boil the eggs

1. Bring a small pot of water to a rolling boil.
2. Gently add the egg.
3. Boil for **6½–7 minutes** for jammy yolks.
4. Transfer immediately to cold water.
5. Peel and slice in half. Set aside.

---

### 2. Pan-fry the chicken

1. Heat a pan over **medium heat** with 1 tsp oil.
2. Add chicken pieces and fry until **golden and crisp on all sides**.
3. Remove from heat and set aside.

---

### 3. Cook the Shin Ramyun

1. In a pot, bring **500–550 ml water** to a boil.
2. Add:
   - Noodles
   - Soup base powder
   - Vegetable flakes

3. Cook for **4–4½ minutes**, stirring occasionally.
4. Let the broth reduce slightly at the end for deeper flavor.

---

### 4. Assemble

1. Pour the noodles and broth into a bowl.
2. Arrange crispy chicken on top.
3. Place egg halves on the side.
4. Garnish with chopped spring onions and sesame seeds.

---

## Notes

- For extra richness, add a small knob of butter or a splash of sesame oil at the end.
- Mushrooms, corn, or spinach work well as add-ins.

Enjoy your bowl of ramen!
