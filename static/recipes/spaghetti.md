# Chilli Garlic Spaghetti

A simple, bold pasta where garlic and chilli shine. Quick to make, comforting to eat.

## Ingredients

- 100–120 g **spaghetti**
- 2 tbsp **olive oil** or **butter**
- 5–6 cloves **garlic**, thinly sliced
- ½ tsp **chilli flakes** (adjust to taste)
- Salt, to taste
- Freshly ground **black pepper**, to taste
- Fresh **parsley** or **spring onions**, finely chopped
- Optional: grated parmesan or a squeeze of lemon

---

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil. Salt it generously.
2. Cook the spaghetti until **al dente**.
3. Reserve **¼ cup pasta water**, then drain the spaghetti.
4. Heat olive oil or butter in a pan over **low heat**.
5. Add sliced garlic and cook gently until fragrant.  
   _Do not let it brown._
6. Add chilli flakes and stir for a few seconds.
7. Add the cooked spaghetti along with a splash of reserved pasta water.
8. Toss well until the pasta is evenly coated.
9. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
10. Turn off the heat and finish with parsley or spring onions.

---

## Serving Notes

- Serve immediately while hot.
- Add parmesan for richness or lemon juice for brightness.
- Works well as a base for mushrooms, shrimp, or vegetables.
