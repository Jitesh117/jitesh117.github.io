# Butter Chicken

A classic North Indian butter chicken — tender chicken cooked in a rich, mildly spiced tomato–butter–cream gravy. Smooth, balanced, and comforting, served best with flaky parathas or naan.

---

## Ingredients (2 servings)

### Chicken Marinade

- 400–500 g **boneless chicken** (thighs or breast), cut into chunks
- ½ cup **dahi (curd)**
- 1 tbsp **ginger-garlic paste**
- 1 tsp **red chilli powder** (Kashmiri preferred)
- ½ tsp **turmeric powder**
- 1 tsp **garam masala**
- Salt, to taste
- 1 tsp **oil or melted butter**

Marinate for **at least 4 hours**, preferably overnight.

---

### Butter Chicken Gravy

- 2 tbsp **butter**
- 1 tbsp **oil**
- 2 cups **tomatoes**, pureed (fresh or canned)
- 1 tbsp **ginger-garlic paste**
- 1 tsp **Kashmiri red chilli powder** (for color)
- 1 tsp **coriander powder**
- 1 tsp **garam masala**
- 10–12 **cashews**, soaked and blended to a paste
- ¼–⅓ cup **fresh cream**
- Salt, to taste
- Sugar, a pinch (optional)
- Dried **kasuri methi**, crushed

---

## Instructions

### 1. Cook the chicken

1. Heat a pan or grill on **medium-high heat**.
2. Add a little oil and cook marinated chicken until lightly charred and just cooked through.
3. Remove and set aside. (The chicken will finish cooking in the gravy)

---

### 2. Prepare the gravy base

1. Heat butter and oil together in a pan on **medium heat**.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant.
3. Add tomato puree and cook for **10–12 minutes**, stirring occasionally, until thick and glossy and butter starts separating.

---

### 3. Spice and enrich

1. Add chilli powder, coriander powder, and salt.
2. Stir in cashew paste and cook for **2–3 minutes** on low heat.
3. Add cooked chicken pieces and mix gently.

---

### 4. Finish the butter chicken

1. Add fresh cream and simmer for **5–7 minutes**.
2. Sprinkle garam masala and crushed kasuri methi.
3. Adjust salt and sweetness if needed.
4. Finish with a small knob of butter on top.

---

## Serve

- Serve hot with paratha, naan, or steamed rice.
- Garnish with cream drizzle or coriander leaves if desired.

---

## Notes

- Kashmiri chilli gives color without too much heat.
- Do not rush cooking the tomato base — this step defines the flavor.
- Butter chicken should be rich but not overly spicy.
